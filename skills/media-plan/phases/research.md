# Research Phase

## Goal

Produce a decision-useful research pack that either supports or rejects the candidate inspiration directions with current, source-backed evidence.

## Required Reads

- `../shared/research-rubric.md`
- `../shared/source-trust-policy.md`
- `../shared/query-playbook.md`

## Rules

- Start only after intake status is `ready`.
- Use `## Inspiration Hints` as hypotheses to verify, not as approved strategy directions.
- Prefer recent sources, usually inside the last 12 months unless the topic moves more slowly.
- Use at least two evidence classes and support each important claim with at least one concrete source.
- Treat platform and social hotspots as signal discovery, then calibrate them with stronger editorial, consulting, or official context when the strategic stakes are high.
- Name unsupported inspiration directions and material risks explicitly.
- Extract only the signals that can sharpen a later proposal, theme, hero event, or conversion path; skip trivia that does not change decisions.

## Required Add-On Output

When inspiration hints or clear direction-selection risk is present, append:

```md
## Inspiration Signal Check
- Supported directions:
- Weak or unsupported directions:
- Risks or cautions:
```

Also append proposal-ready ammunition that later phases can turn into a highlight mechanism:

```md
## Proposal Ammunition
- Audience tension:
- Scene or regional hooks:
- Category cliches to avoid:
- Mechanic-worthy signals:
- Proof assets worth carrying into proposal:
```
