# Competitor Actionable Table

Use during **Research** and carry forward into `Research-Based Insights` in the proposal. Converts “X is strong” into **actionable** strategy inputs.

## Output shape (append to research pack)

```md
## Competitor Actionable View
| Competitor / benchmark | What users trust and why | What they distrust or ignore | Implication for our trust path | Our break / counter-move (comms or experience) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [name] | [evidence-backed] | [evidence-backed or hypothesis stated as such] | [one line] | [named action, no vague “do better”] |
```

## Rules

- Minimum **two** named rows when the brief names competitors; if none named, use **category benchmarks** and label them as such.
- Each row must cite at least one **concrete** source in the research pack (or mark `TBC` and add to Gaps).
- **Reject** for research `pass` if the only competitor analysis is praise (“X is very successful”) with no **trust path** and no **our break** column.
- `Our break / counter-move` must be something the proposal can **schedule** (hero event, content IP, channel proof, trial mechanic).
