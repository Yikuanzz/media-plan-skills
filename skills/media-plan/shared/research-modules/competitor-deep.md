# Research module: competitor deep

**When to load:** Review loop routed to `research` for **evidence** gaps on competitors, or initial research on competitive campaigns.

**Before** running `tavily_search` or other web tools, read this file and [../query-playbook.md](../query-playbook.md) competitor section.

## Query focus

- Brand + `上市` + `传播` + region / year
- Competitor + `营销` + `案例` + platform name
- Category + `社媒` + `话题` for narrative patterns

## Source mix

- Prefer: brand-owned releases, major trade or news coverage, verifiable case writeups.
- Distinguish **paid stunt** vs **sustained program**; both matter for `Competitor Actionable View`.

## Output

- Feed rows in [../competitor-actionable.md](../competitor-actionable.md) first; then consolidate into `## Key Findings`.
