# Source Trust Policy

## Trust Levels

- High trust: government agencies, platform official sources, brand official releases, authoritative research institutions, authoritative industry reports.
- Medium trust: mainstream business media, established industry media, mature commercial database summaries.
- Low trust: marketing copy, repost farms, anonymous summaries, opinion-heavy self-media pages.
- Disallowed: undated, unattributed, unverifiable, or obviously recycled sources.

## Rules

- High-stakes strategic claims should prefer high-trust or medium-high-trust support.
- Low-trust sources can suggest a signal worth checking but should not be the only support for key conclusions.
- If only low-trust sources are available, mark the direction or claim as weak rather than upgrading it into a confident recommendation.
